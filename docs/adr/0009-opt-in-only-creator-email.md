# Creator email is inbound or opt-in only

## Status
accepted

ArcadeBloom will not autonomously email authors merely because their public contact details can be found. During the zero-cost phase, `hello@arcadebloom.com` uses free inbound routing to a verified owner address, and automated correspondence is limited to creators who first contacted the site, submitted a game, subscribed, or explicitly granted permission; the previously accepted unsolicited-notification design in ADR-0005 is superseded. This conforms to the transactional and pricing boundaries of available email infrastructure while preserving automated replies, corrections, takedowns, and consented notifications.

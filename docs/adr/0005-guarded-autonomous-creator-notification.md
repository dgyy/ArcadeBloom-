# Guarded autonomous notification for game creators

## Status
superseded by ADR-0009

ArcadeBloom may autonomously notify a creator only after the creator's game passes automated evidence review and becomes index-eligible. The system may use only an explicitly public project-contact channel, send one transparent and evidence-specific notice without asking for a backlink, and must enforce permanent suppression after a bounce, refusal, complaint, removal request, or opt-out; harvested commit addresses and automated follow-ups are prohibited. This trades outreach volume for sender reputation, creator trust, and a distribution loop that can operate without human approval.
